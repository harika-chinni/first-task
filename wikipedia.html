shanmukh jaswanth

Shanmukh Jaswanth Kandregula is an Indian youtuber, television personality and actor. He is best known for his role as "Shannu" in the YouTube series The Software DevLOVEper and as "Surya" in Surya.[2] As of February 2022, he has over 4.3 million subscribers on his YouTube channel. His other popular videos include The Viva, The Interview and Software Lo Inthe.
